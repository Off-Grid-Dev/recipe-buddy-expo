# Privacy Policy for Recipe Buddy

**Effective Date:** April 4, 2026  
**Last Updated:** April 4, 2026

**Recipe Buddy** ("we", "our", or "us"), based in Norway, is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding information collection for the Recipe Buddy mobile application.

## 1. No Data Collection
The current version of Recipe Buddy does **not collect, store, or transmit any personal information** or user data. 
* All recipes or data you enter are stored locally on your device.
* We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking software.

## 2. Permissions
The app may request standard device permissions necessary for its core functionality (such as storage access to save your recipes locally). These permissions are used only to process data on your device.

## 3. Children's Privacy
Our app does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

## 4. International Users (GDPR)
As we are based in Norway, we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Since no personal data is collected or processed by us, no data is transferred outside of your device.

## 5. Future Updates
This policy applies to the current version of the app. Future versions may include user accounts or image uploads, at which point this policy will be updated to reflect those changes.
